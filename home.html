About Me

My name is Naomi Johnson, I am 28 years old. I am Philly born and raised, I love cheesesteaks, mango water ice, and soft pretzels. I am the youngest of four children. I’m a Graphic Designer by trade, I love everything that has to do with Art. I work part time at a marketing agency, and as a Freelance Designer. I’m also a gym rat, I am in the gym at least four times a week. The gym helps me release a lot of stress, I lift heavy weight I see it as a challenge and that excites me.

I enjoy hanging out with my friends and traveling. Within the last three months I’ve been to the Bahamas, Dominican Republic and London. I am always on the go. I am an avid food chaser, I enjoy trying new foods it doesn’t matter what it is, I was even vegan for about six months just to try vegan food. My favorite food is anything that I know is unhealthy for me but I eat it anyway.
