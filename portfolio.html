About Me

Graphic Designer/Web Developer

My name is Naomi Johnson, I am 29 years old. I am Philly born and raised, I love cheesesteaks, mango water ice, and soft pretzels. I am the youngest of four children. I’m a Graphic Designer by trade, I love everything that has to do with Art. I work part time at a marketing agency, and as a Freelance Designer. I’m also a gym rat, I am in the gym at least four times a week. The gym helps me release a lot of stress, I lift heavy weight I see it as a challenge and that excites me.

I enjoy hanging out with my friends and traveling. Within the last three months I’ve been to the Bahamas, Dominican Republic and London. I am always on the go. I am an avid food chaser, I enjoy trying new foods it doesn’t matter what it is, I was even vegan for about six months just to try vegan food. My favorite food is anything that I know is unhealthy for me but I eat it anyway.

Beyonce Trivia
BEYONCE is a pop/hiphop/rnb Icon. It is only right that to be apart of the "Bey Hive" you have to know all about her. Test your knowledge in this Beyonce Trivia game. The rules are simple; There are Five questions pertaining to the events in Beyonce's life. You have four options, click the correct one and a screen comes up telling you that you are correct, click the wrong answer and you are then told that you are no correct. On the final screen you will see how many you got correct and incorrect and have the option to play again. Enjoy!!! Click Here to Play
GIF Tastic!!
Music makes the world go around. Use this helpful site to view gifs of some of your favorite artist of today and yesterday. I have already provided a few of my own. How to use: 1.Either click the already made buttons or type in an artist and create your own button. When the user clicks on a button, the page will grab 10 static, non-animated gif images from the GIPHY API and place them on the page. When the user clicks one of the still GIPHY images, the gif should animate. If the user clicks the gif again, it should stop playing. The gif will display the rating. Have fun!!! Click Here to Play
